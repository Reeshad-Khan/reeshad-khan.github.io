About

Short, direct, and skimmable.

I design efficient perception-to-planning systems for autonomous platforms, with a focus on BEV representations, radar–vision fusion for adverse conditions, and sensor/optics/model co-design (RAW-to-task). I care about what happens after training: exportability, profiling, quantization, and hitting batch-1 latency targets without fragile behavior.
